Timeless Groove Lab
In Memory Of Nova Angels
October 7, 2024
Full-On Trance, Progressive House

On October 7, 2023, a tragic and unprecedented attack occurred in Israel when terrorist militants launched a large-scale assault from Gaza. The attack began early in the morning with a barrage of rockets targeting multiple cities across Israel. Simultaneously, heavily armed militants infiltrated southern Israeli communities, including the area near the Nova music festival. The Nova festival, an all-night electronic music event held in the Negev Desert, became a major target during the attack. Terrorist gunmen ambushed the festival, killing over 260 attendees and taking others hostage. The massacre at the festival was one of the deadliest single attacks in Israel's history.
